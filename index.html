WHAT IS A COVERIST COLLAB?

They are collaborations (normally organized online) where dancers/singers from over the world can get together to sing/dance their favorite songs. For this website, I will be speaking on behalf of singing collabs.

The term “Coverist”, which combines the word “cover” and ends with the ending “ist”, is what classifies these passionate fans. Typically coverists range in the musical genres of K-Pop, J-Pop, and C-Pop, but can expand further as music is universal.

HOW DO I START?

If you are starting fresh as a Coverist online, it will take some time until you can join in more collaborations as you build your portfolio and experience. To get started, here are some starter points to enter the world of covering.

  • Make sure you can sing the language you want to cover in. You do not have to be fluent, but your pronunciation should be somewhat decent
  • Make sure you have the proper tools: A decent microphone, recording/editing program, and the passion to sing!
  • Join Discord Servers, forum pages, or even keep up with YouTube collab channels where people constantly open collab auditions, drop Google forms for auditions
  • Make good connections/friends with people you’d like to cover with
